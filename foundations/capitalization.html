Capitalization

Capitalization is a writing convention that enhances readability and provides a consistent professional tone. Our system uses Title Case and Sentence case.

Capitalization schemes

Scheme Definition Usage in UI Examples
Title Case Capitalize the first letter of every word except articles, coordinating conjunctions, and short prepositions. Proper terms and branded elements Company Success Plan
Sentence case Capitalize only the first letter of the first word and any proper nouns. Standard for general terminology and labels Numerical aperture, Resize image, New folder

Best practices

  • Prefer sentence case for almost all UI text — easier to read, especially for non-native speakers.
  • Reserve Title Case for branding and specific product names.
  • Align with Material Design and Microsoft Fluent content style guidance.
